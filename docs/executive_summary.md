# Airline Pricing Intelligence - Executive Summary 

## KEY FINDINGS

### 1. Stops vs Price
- Flights with more stops are significantly more expensive.
- Mean price increases from ₹5,024 (non-stop) to ₹17,686 (4 stops).

### 2. Correlation Analysis
- Total Stops (0.60) shows stronger correlation with price than duration (0.51).
- However, Duration and Total Stops have a strong correlation with each other (0.74).
- This suggests that longer the duration, higher is the Price.

### 3. Brand Pricing Strategy
- Premium airlines (Jet Airways Business, Vistara Premium) price significantly above route averages.
- Low-cost carriers (GoAir, SpiceJet) consistently price below.

Random Forest achieved:
